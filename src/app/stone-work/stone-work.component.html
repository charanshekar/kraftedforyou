Stone-Work Embroidery

Hand embroidered khadi silk with gems and raw cut stones
Stone-work embroidery refers to using different colored stones to embellish a garment while making it heavier and richer. While certain attires may have both, embroidery and stone-work, it is not uncommon to find attires with only stone-work that creates the embroidery pattern as well. These stones used are regular, semi-precious and raw-cut stones.
